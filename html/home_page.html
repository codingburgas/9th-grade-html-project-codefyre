Welcome to our Fire Safety System!

This system collects important information about the fire stations in the area, the personnel, and the vehicles used for firefighting and rescue.

Through it, employees can easily report incidents by indicating the location on the map provided by our team.

The system tracks which employee and which vehicle belong to a specific team or fire station.
