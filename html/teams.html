Teams

Firefighters Brigade

Responds to all types of fire emergencies, including building, vehicle, and wildfires. They handle extinguishing fires and rescuing people from burning areas.

Flood Rescue Unit

Specializes in rescue operations during floods, helping evacuate people, providing emergency aid, and securing flooded areas.

Search and Rescue Team

Focuses on locating and saving people in various emergencies like collapsed buildings, wilderness rescues, or trapped situations.

Traffic Incident Response Team

Handles accidents on roads, providing medical aid, rescuing trapped individuals, and securing the accident site for safety.
